# LUSENA Project Brief

## What is LUSENA?

LUSENA is a premium Polish silk e-commerce store built as a custom Shopify theme on top of Dawn (v15.4.1). The store sells silk accessories focused on nighttime beauty routines.

**Positioning:** "The only complete nighttime beauty routine in 22 momme silk — pillowcases, hair protection and accessories from Suzhou, Silk Capital since the 15th century."

**Market:** Polish women (25-50), PL-first, premium positioning. Four target segments:
1. "Seeks quality for years" (25-45) — premium, durable investment
2. "Perfect gift" (22-55) — luxurious, impressive first impression
3. "Hair/skin care" (18-40) — reduced friction, visible results
4. "Minimalist aesthete" (20-50) — refined, timeless design

## Brand identity

- **Name:** LUSENA (always uppercase)
- **Language:** All customer-facing content in Polish; code/comments in English
- **Tone:** Premium but approachable, no exclamation marks in headings, sentence case
- **Archetype:** Mentor/Caregiver with Alchemist nuance (knowledge + transformation through material)
- **Personality:** Expert, Empathetic, Subtly luxurious, Precise, Calm
- **Key messaging:** proof-first, "nocna rutyna piękna" framing, benefit language ("wake without creases")
- **Social proof:** NEVER fabricate counts/ratings/reviews; use neutral alternatives

## Tone of voice rules

- **Emphasize:** 22 momme density, tested quality, origin (Suzhou/Shengze), OEKO-TEX certification, local stock & 24h shipping
- **Avoid:** "Made in China" (use "from Suzhou"), "Factory" (use "manufactory"), medical claims, price wars
- **Key claim:** "22 momme to ponad 15% więcej jedwabiu niż standard 19 momme" (22÷19 ≈ 15.8%; qualitative alternative: "Gęstszy splot, dłuższa trwałość")
- **CTAs (Polish):** "Sprawdź kolekcję", "Dodaj do koszyka", "Zobacz więcej"
- **Value anchors:** "0,XX zł/noc" framing when price data is available
