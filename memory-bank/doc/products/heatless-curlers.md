# Jedwabny wałek do loków (Heatless Curlers)

*Last updated: 2026-03-15*
*Copy finalized: 2026-03-15 (creative session complete - material correction: 22 momme 6A silk confirmed)*

> **Note:** This product uses the same 22 momme, 6A grade mulberry silk as all other LUSENA
> products, but has a PP cotton filling inside. It needs custom care instructions due to
> the filling. Fewer universal fields apply - see details below.
>
> **Title recommendation:** Research of the Polish market (Allegro, Silky State, So Fluffy,
> LoveSilk, Mexmo, Cat&Cat) confirmed that "lokówka" means heated curling iron in Polish.
> Zero premium Polish silk brands use "lokówki" for this product. The entire market uses
> "wałek" (roller). Recommended title: **"Jedwabny wałek do loków"**. This breaks LUSENA's
> noun-first pattern (cf. "Poszewka jedwabna") but matches market convention and sounds
> natural. Alternative (pattern-consistent): "Wałek jedwabny do loków" - grammatically
> correct but less natural.

|------|------|---------------|
