Two design concepts for cart upsells

Both types (cross-sell + bundle upgrade) shown in drawer and cart page contexts. Click to select your preference.

A

Unified card - accent border for bundles

Same card structure for both. Bundle gets a teal left border, savings badge, and primary CTA. Cross-sell stays clean with outline button. Minimal visual difference - they clearly belong together.

Strengths

  • Visually cohesive - same card, same rhythm
  • Teal accent subtly signals "this is special"
  • Compact - works well in tight drawer
  • Savings badge draws eye without overwhelming

Trade-offs

  • Bundle upgrade button is small - might get overlooked
  • "+ 260 zl" on a small button - less room to explain the value
  • May underplay the savings opportunity
B

Expanded card - upgrade row for bundles

Cross-sell keeps the compact card. Bundle card grows taller with a dedicated upgrade row below the product info - separated by a hairline. More room for savings messaging and a larger CTA. The card itself remains white, contained, premium.
